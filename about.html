Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ's

What is Bibimbap?
Bibimbap is a healthy korean dish served as a mixed bowl of warm white rice, meat, vegetables and spicy sauce.
What is Bulgogi?
Bulgogi is thin slices of grilled marinated ribeye.
What is Kimchi?
Kimchi is spicy fermented & pickled cabbage.
Do you have any vegetarian options?
Yes, we have veggie, tofu, and kimchi bibimbap. We also have the option to replace the chicken with fried tofu from our Asian Chicken Salad.
Do you do catering?
Yes, we are happy to Cater! We only do pickup orders. Please email us at bibimbap-king@gmail.com
